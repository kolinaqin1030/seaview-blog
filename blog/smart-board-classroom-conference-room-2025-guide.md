## Introduction
Smart boards have become essential tools in both classrooms and conference rooms.  
However, their usage scenarios, feature focus, and hardware configurations differ significantly.

### In Classrooms
- Designed for **interactive learning**
- Multi-touch support for group participation
- Integrates with educational software
- Durable anti-glare glass and safe materials for students

### In Conference Rooms
- Focus on **professional collaboration**
- Supports screen mirroring (Windows, macOS, Android)
- Built-in video conferencing compatibility (Zoom, Teams)
- Sleek design suitable for modern offices
